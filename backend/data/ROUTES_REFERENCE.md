## Class Abbreviations
- **1A**: First AC (₹4,800 - ₹7,100)
- **2A**: Second AC (₹2,500 - ₹5,200)
- **3A**: Third AC (₹1,800 - ₹4,600)
- **SL**: Sleeper (₹400 - ₹1,400)
- **CC**: Chair Car (₹700 - ₹1,800)
- **EC**: Executive Chair Car (₹2,800 - ₹3,200)
- **2S**: Second Seating (₹250)
